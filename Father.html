Fernando T. Sol Jr

My Strong Padre De La Familia

August 17, 1968

Dear Dad,

Hello my Idol! Dad, thank you for all of the hardwork that you did just to ensure that our family doesn't suffer. You are the main reason why I'm studying in La Salle right now, why we are all surviving and eating. You're the reason, we all have experienced to travel to different countries and experienced having gadgets.You experienced a lot of stress and struggles, but then you didn't give up for our future. That's why you are my idol dad, you didn't give up to make our lives stable, yet you don't lose time for us and I want to do the same thing when I grow up. Our happiness is always your happiness, you always put us first before you and we really appreciate it dad! Thank you for being a simple, kind and humble father that I aspire to be. I'm sorry if I'm a pain in the ass sometimes and I know every time you scold me, you are just trying to teach me a lesson. Yes dad, those life lessons are very effective, it really helped me and developed me as a responsible and mature person. Please dad and also mom, don't stop guiding and teaching me and kuya with life lessons, we have a lot more to learn from you guys. I just wish this Christmas Dad, just relax and have fun. I wish you good health Dad! Don't worry Dad, me and kuya will never get tired of taking care of you and Mom. I promise to buy you a big rest house at the future, just what you really want, just to relax and be with your loved ones. I love you so much Dad, you are the best Dad for me! Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year!

Love your Son,
Frando Sol
