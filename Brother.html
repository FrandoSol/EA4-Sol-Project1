My Macho Big Brother

May 25, 1994

Dear Big brother,

Hello Big bro! WASSUP! First of all, I want to thank you for being a good big brother to me, I am very lucky to have a mature and cool big brother. You and I have the same trip big bro! That's why we are close! Thank you for always having my back and always ready to help when I need some help in academics and other stuff. I'm sorry big brother for being stubborn a lot of times and a bully to you. We always fight, mostly because I always annoy you and I'm really sorry about it. But even we always fight, we always make sure that we'll be okay soon. I'm gonna be honest sometimes we don't like each other's attitude because sometimes one of us is not in the mood to talk or something, it's part of life big bro, the important thing is we realized that we're affecting other people. Big bro, let's promise each other that we will always help each other no matter what happens and we will take care of our parents until the end! Thank you for guiding me always whenever mom and dad are not around! I just wish this Christmas that you'll have a girlfriend HAHA! I love you big brother!

Love your Little Brother,
Frando Sol
