Dear Mom,

Hello my dearest mom! First of all I want to thank you for all of the love and care that you gave me, no one in this world can beat that. Thank you for always being there for me when I'm sad or just feeling down especially when I feel such a failure when I didn't achieve my goal, mostly in academics. Thank you for always reminding me to don't give up and be better next time like in my quizzes. I'm sorry if most of the time I'm a stubborn child. I'm sorry if I hurt you mom, especially with my words and my tone when I am angry or pissed at something. I didn't mean those things, I always love you! I'm sorry if me and kuya always fight, yet you are still there to fix our problem. Even if it's our problem, you are there to help us. You have a pure heart Mom, no wonder Dad fell in love with you. Me and kuya promise to take care of you and Dad forever, we will never get tired of giving back the love,care and sacrificethat you two gave us. Mom, I will always be your baby boy no matter what! I love you so much. I just wish this Christmas, that all of us are full of laughter and joy, I wish you good health and I promise at the future I would buy you a lot of Pandoras, HAHA, your fave! Me,kuya and Dad promise to let you feel our love everyday! Oh and one thing Mom! Take it easy on the Kdrama stuff! You're really addicted! HAHA I LOVE YOU SO MUCH! Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year!

Love your Son,
Frando Sol
